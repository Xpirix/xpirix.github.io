Expected Results

The name given to the tool at this stage is the "Sanitation Geoportal of the Antananarivo agglomeration". It will be a web portal dedicated to sanitation stakeholders to facilitate decision-making on urban water management. It will also be open to citizens to facilitate risk management and the dissemination of alerts related to water management. It will have a simple, modern, and intuitive interface following current standards to allow users to adapt quickly.

Main Pages: Map and Layers

The map tab, as its name suggests, will be equipped with a viewer that allows interactive display of geographic data integrated into the Geoportal. These data will first be classified by themes, then for each chosen theme, the corresponding layers list will be displayed. For this photo, for example, the layers related to the SDA of the C UA in the water and sanitation theme are displayed.

Interaction with Data

Interaction with the data on the map will be defined by a click on an entity. This action will display all the attribute information of the entity as a descriptive sheet as shown in this photo. For example, the reinforcement work of the 67Ha pumping station was planned for the first horizon, in 2018.
